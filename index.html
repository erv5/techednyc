Our Mission

We aim to provide underprivileged students in NYC with the skills and mentorship needed for future tech careers.

Our Programs

Coding

Learn foundational and advanced programming skills, from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and Python.

Cyber Security

Understand online safety, data protection, and strategies to protect against cyber threats.

Cryptocurrency

Explore cryptocurrency basics, blockchain, and financial literacy for the future of finance.

About Me

As a first-generation American from Brooklyn, I’m dedicated to providing students with the mentorship and skills I wish I’d had growing up. With experience in Cyber Security, Systems Administration, and Software Development, I bring expertise and passion to empower NYC youth.
